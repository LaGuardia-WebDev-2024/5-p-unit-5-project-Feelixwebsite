An in depth explanation of Life is Strange 2

I would go as far as to call myself a choice based story game connoisseur- with my credentials being my completion of Detroit Become Human, The Walking Dead, Untill Dawn, and many others.

However- no game ive played so far has come close to the masterpiece that is Life is strange 2. It's dramatic with an interesting concept, lovable characters and a fantastic adition to the universe the original sory takes place in.

To understand the second game, you need some context from the first.

Life is strange, the begining

The original story takes place in a small town in Oregon called Arcadia Bay.You play as Max Caufield, an 18 year old photography student who finds out she has the ability to travel back in time. She rekindles her friendship with her childhood best friend, Chloe Price, and together they begin their search for a mysterious missing girl, Rachel Amber.

Throughout the game you make decisions that effect how Max moves through the story. Whenever you make an important choice, a butterfly appears in the top right corner with text underneath reading "This action will have consequences." If you arent sure about your choice, you can rewind and try again. But, you wont know how the choice effects the story until later.

Overall, the game is about dealing with the consequences of your actions, and is a reminder that you only have one shot, you cant just undo it.

Life is strange 2: Characters

Let's meet the characters! We have our two main characters, and a plethora of side characters, some with more relevance then others.

Sean

This is the protagonist, Sean Diaz. He is of Mexican (Confirmed, Fathers side) and possibly Irish (Speculated, Mothers side) ancestry. He starts the game as a 16 year old boy preparing for a party with his best friend and the girl he likes. He is a creative, sensative, protective older brother with a love for drawing thats shown in the game through acheivments earned when you sit down to draw. Keeping track of his adventures through his sketchbook, he moves through the world trying his best to keep his brother safe on the run, with a relative moral compass. During the story, he faces challenges like racism, religious cults, and the ongoing threat of being caught by the police and seperated from his brother. Through the game you watch him grow into adulthood, and shape his personality and interactions depending on the choices you make.

Daniel

Meet Daniel Diaz, Sean's little brother. He is a mischevious, trusting, and curious little boy with a knack for atrracting trouble. However, he isnt a normal kid- he has telekinetic powers, and no clue how to use them. While on the run, he trains his skills and must decide wether to use them for good or in some cases, distruction. Based on the choices you make as Sean, Daniel can either be molded into a force of good, or a dangerous weapon.

Karen and Esteban

These two are Karen and Esteban, the parents of the Diaz brothers. Esteban Diaz is a Mexican American man living with his sons in Seattle, and a kind and loving father. He dislikes lies, as shown when you get a choice to either lie to him or tell the truth. Unfortunatley, he is killed off early in the first episode.

Karen Reynolds on the other hand is a caring but flighty woman, who leaves the family before the events of the game. She is Esteban's ex-wife, and while she loves him and her children dearly, she felt she wasn't meant to be a wife or a mother. Sean hates her for this, all the way until you recconect with her in episode 4, where you can choose wether or not to forgive her.

Finn and Cassidy

Next, we have Finn and Cassidy, Seans potential love intrests. After meeting them breifly in chapter 2, Sean and Daniel join their ragtag group of friends and train hop all the way to a pot farm in the Redwoods of California- where chapter 3 takes place. In this chapter, you can choose to either Romance Finn or Cassidy. Both of them are laid back and extroverted, with Finn being on the more adventurous side.

Some of the less relevant side characters:

Lyla

Lyla is Sean's best friend forever. The player meets her in the very first episode, but she ends up only being in the game for a few minutes. She is loyal and caring- and she never stops looking for Sean and his brother after they go missing.

Chris

Meet Chris- an optimistic and imaginative little boy who after meeting Sean and Daniel, becomes convinced he has super powers. He lives with his single and emotionally constipated father, and spends his days playing his super alter ego- capitain spirit (as shown in the " The adventures of capitain spirit" spinoff game).

Jacob

This is Jacob, a character Sean and Daniel meet in episode 3. He grew up in a religious cult, and after escaping found himself in California with the rest of the burnouts.
